LOCATION - Solf, Finland

Solf is a small village on the west coast of Finland. The nearest city is Vaasa which is around 20km away and the closest that public transportation will bring you

GETTING THERE

The smooth option - Fly to Helsinki and take the train to Vaasa

Fly into Helsinki - we recommend Finnair or KLM

Book a train ticket through VR - Railways from Helsinki airport to Vaasa

From the airport you take a 8 minute metro train (P train) to Tikkurila, where you transfer to the intercity train to Vaasa


The commuter train time is flexible, while the intercity is not. Take an earlier commuter train than the time indicated on the ticket to not miss the connection. The train takes around 4h and has plenty of amenities (restaurant, playground, sockets). Book a morning or early afternoon flight to be able to take a train the same day.

The quick option - Fly to Vaasa with a layover

Vaasa also has an airport!

However there are not many connections, so be prepared for a layover

Accomodation

Accomodation is very dependent on transportation. If you have a car, Solf Gästgivargård is a good option. If you don't have a car, we recommend staying in a hotel in Vaasa. We will arrange a taxi/bus going from Vaasa to the wedding location early afternoon and back to Vaasa at the end of the night.

Solf Gästgivargård

This Bed&Breakfast is the only place to stay in Solf. It is only 500 meters from the wedding location. A double room (2 people) costs 70 euros per night, breakfast included. The website is not available in English, but we are happy to help.
